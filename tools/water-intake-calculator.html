Why Hydration Matters

Water makes up about 60% of your body. Proper hydration is essential for virtually every function in your body.

Benefits of Proper Hydration

  • Regulates body temperature and blood pressure
  • Supports digestion and nutrient absorption
  • Improves energy levels and brain function
  • Flushes toxins and supports kidney function
  • Keeps skin healthy and joints lubricated

Signs of Dehydration

  • ● Dark yellow or amber-colored urine
  • ● Persistent headaches and dizziness
  • ● Fatigue, confusion, and irritability
  • ● Dry mouth, lips, and skin
  • ● Muscle cramps and reduced exercise performance

Tips to Drink More Water

  • Carry a reusable water bottle everywhere
  • Set hourly reminders on your phone
  • Add lemon, cucumber, or mint for flavor
  • Drink a glass before every meal
  • Start and end your day with a full glass
